# Lead Marketplace SaaS

## Overview

Lead Marketplace is a SaaS concept designed to connect homeowners with local service professionals.

The platform focuses on lead capture, lead preview, credit-based purchasing, and role-based dashboards for professionals.

## Problem

Local contractors often struggle with:

- Expensive lead platforms
- Low-quality leads
- Poor transparency
- Inefficient follow-up
- Lack of workflow automation

## Solution

The concept includes:

- Homeowner lead submission
- Professional dashboard
- Lead preview
- Credit-based lead purchase
- Stripe payment flow
- Supabase backend
- Role-based access control

## AI Relevance

This project shows how AI can support:

- Lead classification
- Lead quality scoring
- Automated summaries
- Professional matching
- Customer support workflows
- Sales automation
