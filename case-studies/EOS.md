# EOS — Emergency Operating System

## Overview

EOS is a bilingual emergency decision platform designed to help families make better decisions during crisis situations.

The idea is to create a system that can guide users through stressful scenarios using clear questions, structured decision paths, and AI-assisted recommendations.

## Problem

During emergencies, people often struggle with:

- Stress
- Poor communication
- Lack of structure
- Conflicting information
- Limited connectivity
- Unclear next steps

## Solution

EOS was designed as a decision-support system that helps families:

- Understand the situation
- Follow structured steps
- Coordinate actions
- Reduce confusion
- Make safer decisions
