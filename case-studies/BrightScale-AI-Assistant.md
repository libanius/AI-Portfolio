# BrightScale AI Assistant

## Overview

BrightScale AI Assistant is an AI-powered sales and customer service concept for a home services company.

The assistant is designed to qualify leads, answer customer questions, guide pricing expectations, recommend services, and escalate complex situations to a human.

## Problem

Home services businesses often lose leads because of:

- Slow response time
- Inconsistent pricing communication
- Poor follow-up
- Missed upsell opportunities
- Repetitive customer questions

## Solution

The assistant supports:

- Lead qualification
- Customer intake
- Service recommendations
- Pricing guidance
- Scheduling support
- Multilingual communication
- Escalation to a human when needed

## AI Relevance

This project demonstrates practical AI agent design for real business operations.

Key AI components include:

- System prompt design
- Business-rule enforcement
- Conversation flow planning
- Qualification logic
- Tone control
- Multilingual customer support
